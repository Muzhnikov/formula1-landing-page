About the Team

Red Bull Racing, competing as Oracle Red Bull Racing, is one of the most successful teams in modern F1 history. Known for their aerodynamic innovation and dominant performances, they've revolutionized the sport with concepts like the blown diffuser and advanced energy recovery systems.

The team holds multiple records including most wins in a season (21 in 2023) and longest winning streak (15 races in 2023).

About the Team

Scuderia Ferrari is the oldest and most successful team in Formula 1 history. Competing in every championship since 1950, Ferrari is more than a racing team - it's an institution in motorsport. The passionate tifosi (Ferrari fans) create an atmosphere like no other at every Grand Prix.

Ferrari's factory in Maranello is a state-of-the-art facility where road cars and F1 cars are developed side by side, creating a unique synergy between racing and production vehicles.

About the Team

Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One Team dominated the hybrid era with unprecedented success. From 2014 to 2021, they won 8 consecutive Constructors' Championships, setting new standards for technical excellence and team operations.

The team's success was built on their innovative power unit technology and a culture of relentless improvement. Their "no-blame" culture and data-driven approach became the blueprint for modern F1 teams.

About the Team

McLaren Racing is one of the most successful teams in F1 history with a legacy of innovation. Founded by Bruce McLaren, the team introduced groundbreaking technologies including the first carbon fiber composite chassis in 1981.

Known for their iconic papaya orange livery, McLaren combines cutting-edge technology with a rich racing heritage. Their factory in Woking features the McLaren Technology Centre, an architectural marvel designed by Norman Foster.
